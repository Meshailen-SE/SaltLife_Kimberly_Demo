SaltLife Camps Bay offers exceptional luxury and comfort in one of Cape Town's most prestigious locations. Designed to feel like a private home, this self-catering, owner-run property provides a serene and secluded atmosphere with easy living, perfectly located within 300m of Camps Bay's iconic white beaches and cosmopolitan seaside village.

Four beautifully appointed king bedrooms, each with private ensuite bathroom, TV, and mood lighting ensure unparalleled relaxation. With uninterrupted power supply and Alexa home automation, modern convenience meets coastal elegance.
